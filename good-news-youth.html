About

A place to belong and grow

Middle school and high school are some of the most formative years of a person's life — and we take that seriously. Good News Youth is a place where students can be real about their questions, their doubts, and their faith. We don't have a polished program. We have a community.

Twice a month we gather for a Christ-centered lesson and honest conversation. The kind where students are actually heard — not just talked at. The goal is simple: help teenagers know Jesus and follow him in the middle of everything else going on.

Discipleship

Community that goes beyond Sunday night

Our leaders are committed adults who show up, listen well, and walk alongside students through everything that comes with growing up. The relationships built here tend to stick. This isn't a drop-off youth group — it's a place where students are known, challenged, and sent out with a clearer sense of who they are in Christ.

For parents: This ministry is designed to partner with you, not replace you. We want to reinforce what you're already building at home and keep you in the loop.
