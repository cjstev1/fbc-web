Welcome

A place built for kids

Good News Kids is our Wednesday night ministry for children in Kindergarten through 5th grade. We believe the gospel is for everyone — including the youngest hearts in our church. Every week is designed to be fun, engaging, and grounded in the truth of Scripture. Kids aren't just sitting through a program here; they're growing.

Our leaders love kids by name and show up week after week to invest in them. Whether your child is brand new or has been with us for years, they will find a place here.

What Wednesday looks like

Fun, focused, and full of truth

A typical Wednesday night starts with games and music, moves into an age-appropriate Bible lesson, and wraps up with small group discussion time. We keep things interactive so kids stay engaged — and so the lesson actually lands. Each week connects to a bigger picture of who God is and what he has done.
